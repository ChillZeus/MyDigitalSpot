My thoughts on Minecraft 1.17

1.17 was announced a little while ago and it is set to come out this summer. I am looking forward to this new update for a few reasons. I think the lush caves will make caving, something that has been in the game since the beginning, a more interesting and varied experience. I think using come of those new massive caves to build survival bases will be pretty cool and I think people could do some cool stuff with it. I don't know if I like the new wardens though, I think those could make caving too dangerous. I think one thing that is nice about caving is how it's relaxing: there is the occasional mob and lava pit but it's not like there is some dangerous boss down there.

I do not like the new stalactites and stalagmites, though. I think those do NOT feel like vanilla minecraft and though I know people always say it feels modded after something is released, this one really does. I like the new copper blocks and such, I think having blocks that look weathered could be quite cool.

Now for the cliffs side of the update: I like it! I think minecraft has been missing that for a while now because they have "hills' and "cliffs" right now but no mountains. Mountain goats could be pretty cool too, making the once easy task of climbing a hill/mountain a bit harder. I'm all for little changes like this that make a game that people have gotten so good at a little harder. And for the new other stuff like candles, I think that will be cool too. We will never have enough lighting options! So all in all I like how 1.17 is sounding, and I am ready for it.
